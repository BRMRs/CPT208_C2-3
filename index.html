Welcome to Go Climbers

Go Climbers is a mobile and wearable app designed for the climbing community. It enables climbers to find partners at their skill level, book certified coaches, discover nearby climbing venues, and track their performance using wearable devices.

Bio

Emma discovered indoor climbing three months ago at a friend's birthday event and now visits a local bouldering gym once a week. She typically climbs alone, attempts familiar beginner routes, then hesitates to try harder problems. She records workouts in Apple Health but her climbing activity is manually logged with limited detail. On busy weeks, she skips climbing entirely because coordinating with friends takes too long. With Go Climbers, Emma can find beginner partners nearby, book coaches directly, and track her progress with her smartwatch — all in one place.

  • Deliver measurable, data-driven coaching feedback
  • Expand client base through professional discovery
  • Build stronger client retention with milestones and challenges
  • Reduce admin overhead and protect coaching energy

Frustrations

  • Managing bookings across two venues via chat apps causes conflicts
  • Student progress is fragmented across spreadsheets and apps
  • No centralized professional profile for client discovery
  • Wearable data trapped in separate ecosystems

Bio

Marcus splits his week between two climbing gyms, coaching 15-20 clients ranging from beginners to competition-level intermediates. His booking communication happens through WhatsApp and WeChat, while attendance is tracked in spreadsheets. He asks clients to share wearable screenshots to build feedback, but the data is siloed. He posts technique clips online but lacks a professional discovery channel. With Go Climbers, Marcus can publish one profile, sync slots across gyms, access unified wearable data, and run route challenges to keep clients engaged between sessions.
